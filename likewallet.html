LikeWallet

Marketers pay big $$$ to influencers on social media to get their products promoted, but it's hard for them to know what they're getting in return for their investment.

I made LikeWallet to try and solve that problem. The web app connects with Instagram to track the posts of influencers of your choice. Not only can you view how many likes and views their posts are getting, but you can also get demographic information about the people seeing the posts, as well as how many of them are clicking through to your website.

Roles and Achievements

I interviewed 20 marketers to understand how influencer marketing works across different industries. I then persistently engaged five marketers in the e-commerce industry to design a solution tailored to their needs, securing a $10k annual offer for a minimum viable product.

I designed a landing page and explainer video to communicate the value proposition of the product, and then launched a pre-registration campaign to analyze market demand. In two weeks, the page gathered 70 business sign-ups, further validating the demand for the project.

I designed an interactive user interface centered around data visualization by using wireframes and mockups to communicate my design ideas to target users. I also conducted five usability tests with a high-fidelity prototype to resolve design complexities. As a final touch, I Implemented Google's Material Design language across the interface.
