Lean Launchpad

In the spring of 2017 I completed the Lean Launchpad class taught by Steve Blank at the Haas School of Business at Berkeley. This is a competitive class in which a select number of candidate teams are chosen by experienced entrepreneurial professsors to learn the lean startup methodology and validate several business ideas throughout the semester.

Roles and Achievements

I led two experiments and 145 interviews to test 100+ hypotheses and validate three business ideas. I learned how to ask targeted questions to target users and communicate my ideas through wireframes to quickly collect evidence to validate or change my assumptions. Every week I presented my findings to my classmates, the teaching class, and invited industry experts, to answer their questions and defend my decisions.

I created and evolve three different business models using the Strategyzer business model canvas. I formed value proposition diagrams to clearly link the needs of target users to the values of my business ideas. I also conducted three market and competitor analyses to further assess the business opportunities
