Yipp

I made Yipp because I was tired of never finding the links I sent to my teammates! It's a chrome extension that let's teams bookmark pages as a group so they can do online research together more efficiently. Users can comment and discuss directly on a web page, @mention their teammates, and see what pages everyone is bookmarking. Yipp is currently in Beta in the chrome store.
